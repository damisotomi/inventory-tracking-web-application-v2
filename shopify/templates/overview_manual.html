OVERVIEW

This web application allows you perform CRUD (create, read, update and delete) operations for products,
warehouses, transactions and product groups.

It also allows you assign products to different warehouses and allows you view the list of products across all
warehouses combined.

Navigation Links

On the left side of the screen, a list of dropdown links that direct you to different parts of the web app is shown.

You can click on the "Products" dropdown link to direct you to a list of products or to add a product.

Similarly, you can do the same for Transactions, Warehouses, Groups by clicking on their respective drop down links.
