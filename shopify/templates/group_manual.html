Group Manual

Adding Product Groups

You can add a new product group by clicking on "Add a Group" .

Editing Product Group

To edit a product group, Navigate to "Group List" , click on the product group name
click on edit and update the product group.

Deleting Product Group

To Delete a product group, Navigate to "Group List" , click on the product group name,
click on delete and confirm

Note: When you delete a group, all products under that group will have their "product group" column set to blank.
