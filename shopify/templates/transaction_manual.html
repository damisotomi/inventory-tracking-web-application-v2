Transactions Manual

Adding a Transaction

Once you have added details of a product, you can begin to add transactions for that product.

Adding transactions allows you to assign quantities of a product to various warehouses.

You can add a new transaction by clicking on "Add a transaction"

Editing Transaction

To edit a transaction, Navigate to "Transaction list" click on the transaction, click on edit and update the transaction.

Deleting transactions

To Delete a transaction, Navigate to "Transaction list", click on the transaction, click on delete and confirm
